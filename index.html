History and Historical Sites of Seoul

This graph shows the growth of Seoul as a city and population throughout the years, and how it is thriving more than ever. Check out this website to know more about the history and statistics of Seoul. The History of Seoul


Changdeokgung Palace is located to the east of Gyeongbokgung Palace, and it is also known as Donggwol, the Eastern Palace. The castle has been home to the government of Joseon for 270 years and was also the favorite residence of many kings of the Joseon Dynasty. The Changdeokgung Palace is the royal residential palace with the most extended service. Changdeokgung Palace is well-preserved relative to other castles and still has many of its original features.
